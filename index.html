Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our platform

How accurate is your AI detection technology?

Our AI detection technology achieves an accuracy rate of 99.4% in identifying AI-generated content across major language models including GPT-4, Claude, Llama, and Gemini. We continuously train our models on the latest AI outputs to maintain this high accuracy rate.

What types of AI-generated content can you detect?

Our system can detect content generated by all major AI models, including GPT-3.5/4, Claude, Llama, Bard/Gemini, and others. We can identify AI-generated articles, essays, product descriptions, marketing copy, and virtually any text-based content, even when it's been edited or combined with human writing.

How does your pricing model work?

We offer flexible subscription plans based on usage volume, starting with a free tier that allows up to 1,000 words per day. Our professional plans start at $29/month for 100,000 words, and we offer custom enterprise solutions for high-volume needs. All plans include access to our core detection technology, with additional features available at higher tiers.

Do you support languages other than English?

Yes! We currently support 12 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and Hindi. Our multi-language detection maintains similar accuracy rates across all supported languages, and we're continually adding more languages.

How easy is it to integrate with your API?

Our API is designed for simple integration with comprehensive documentation, code samples in multiple languages (Python, JavaScript, PHP, Java, Ruby), and dedicated support. Most developers can implement basic AI detection functionality in under an hour. We also offer pre-built plugins for WordPress, Shopify, and other popular platforms.

Can the system be tricked or bypassed?

While no detection system is 100% foolproof, our technology uses multiple detection methods and continuous learning to stay ahead of evasion techniques. We can detect content that has been modified to avoid detection, including text that has been paraphrased, edited by humans after AI generation, or processed through "anti-detection" tools.
