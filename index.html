Understanding AI Detection Scores

Our AI detection tool analyzes text to determine the likelihood that it was generated by AI or written by a human. Here's how to interpret the results:

Confidence Score

A score of 90% human means we are 90% confident the text was written by a human, not that 90% of the text is human-written.

Sentence Highlighting

Each sentence is highlighted based on our confidence that it was AI-generated or human-written.

AI Model Detection

When possible, we identify which specific AI model likely generated the content.

Detailed Metrics

We analyze multiple factors like writing style, patterns, complexity, and perplexity.

How It Works

Our tool combines multiple detection techniques to accurately identify AI-generated content:

  • Statistical pattern analysis
  • Language model perplexity measures
  • Stylometric analysis
  • Sentence structure and complexity evaluation
  • Token predictability assessment
